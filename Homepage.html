Welcome! Barangay Caniogan

Mission

By delivering efficient public services, encouraging social inclusion, and safeguarding the well-being of its citizens, Barangay Caniogan is devoted to building a safe and prosperous community.

Vision

Barangay Caniogan sees a future in which inhabitants have a good quality of life marked by sustainable development, equal opportunities, and a strong feeling of belonging within a peaceful community.

Core Values

Barangay Caniogan embraces the ideals of honesty, transparency, and community participation, trying to establish an atmosphere in which citizens cooperate, appreciate diversity, and actively contribute to the barangay's overall growth and welfare.
